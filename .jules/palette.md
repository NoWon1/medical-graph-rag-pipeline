## 2024-05-24 - Focus Colors in Medical Apps
**Learning:** Avoid using alarming colors like red (e.g., `#e53935`) for standard interaction states like focus rings on inputs, especially in medical/healthcare applications where red can signal a critical error or danger to users.
**Action:** Always use brand colors (like green `#2fa36b`) or standard neutral blues for focus rings to ensure interactions feel safe and expected.
